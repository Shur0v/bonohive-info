Expiry Date

Pure honey has an extremely long shelf life and does not have an expiry date. Due to its low moisture content and acidic pH, honey has natural preservative properties that inhibit the growth of bacteria, yeast, and molds. When stored properly in a sealed container at room temperature, honey can remain stable and safe to consume indefinitely. Over time, honey may crystallize or change in texture, but this does not indicate spoilage. To maintain the quality of honey, it is recommended to store it in a cool, dry place and avoid introducing moisture or contaminants into the container. If stored properly, pure honey can be enjoyed for years without any significant degradation in taste, quality, or safety.
